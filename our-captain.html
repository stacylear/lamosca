    CAPTAIN JUAN

    If it's your bucket list fish you're after, or if you desire to cast a fly to one of the many Pacific or Sea of Cortez pelagic species that roam the sub-tropical waters surrounding Cabo San Lucas Mexico, then going on a trip with Captain Juan will not disappoint. Captain Juan is an accomplished fly fishing guide and fly fisherman who knows where to take you to the species you're looking to hook up with. He is an expert and unmatched at boating and maneuvering for the bait, teasing for the cast, hook up, then fighting the fish to the boat or release that is just darn fun to watch and be a part of. As a 27-year licensed Boat and Fishing Captain, born and raised in Acapulco in a family tradition of commercial fishermen, he is a truly experienced professional in the Cabo Fishing arena. Captain Juan started fishing at the early age of 8 years old. He moved to Cabo San Lucas 24 years ago and started plying his trade moving into the evolving fly fishing enthusiast client charters. Captain Juan is fluent in English, is a loving family man, and a true sportsman promoting catch and release of endangered and non-edible species. Captain Juan operates the La Mosca, a 26-foot panga, out of the Cabo marina. With 19 years of his venture into fly fishing charters Captain Juan has mastered the techniques necessary to put you into the position to cast to your bucket list pelagic!

    ROBERTO RAMIREZ

    Roberto Rameriz has lived in Cabo San Lucas for the last 17 years, and has been an avid fly fishing and light tackle guide for over 15 years. Roberto is an excellent mate on the 26-foot super panga La Mosca, with his brother, Captain Juan, at the helm. Originally from Acapulco, Roberto started fishing with his family of commercial fishermen and has continued learning and teaching the trade. Since arriving in Cabo San Lucas he has been actively engaged in the off shore and inshore fishing guide service. Roberto speaks Spanish and English. He is a tireless worker on the La Mosca, setting up the baits and lures for teasing baits for the fly caster or for the light tackle fisherman.
